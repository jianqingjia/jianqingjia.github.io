I am a Math PhD candidate at Syracuse University, fortunately advised by Prof. Lixin Shen.

I received my Master’s degree in computational mathematics from the joint program held by Math Department at Hebei Normal University and CS department at Temple University, advised by Prof. Wei Guo and Prof. Haibin Ling.

My research mainly focus on the field of optimization and machine learning, particularly convex and nonconvex optimization, compressed sensing, scientific computing, signal and image processing, augmented reality.

SU email

Your @syr.edu account can be accessed from a web browser as well as from an email client. The web address is  https://sumail.syr.edu/

The account can forward messages to another email address. However, some students report that not all their incoming mail is being forwarded. Thus, you should still check your @syr.edu email daily .
